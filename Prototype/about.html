Who am I?

Hello, I'm Ben Tito Kyalo!

I'm an Electrical and Electronics Engineer and IT Consultant with a strong passion for Open Source Software and DevOps. My journey combines technical expertise in both hardware and software, allowing me to bridge the gap between engineering and technology.

With experience in server administration, database management, and web development, I specialize in creating integrated solutions that leverage the best of both electrical engineering and information technology.

I'm currently pursuing my BSc in Electrical Engineering at the University of Nairobi, where I'm also involved in exciting projects like developing an AI-powered WhatsApp healthcare chatbot using NLP and LLMs, focused on improving healthcare literacy in LMICs.
